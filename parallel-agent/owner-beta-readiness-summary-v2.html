Component: owner-beta-readiness-summary-v2.html
Pre-beta launch checklist + scoring. 5 categories (Quality/Performance/Content/Privacy/Sign-off = 100). Beta threshold = 80. Full release threshold = 90. 3–5 pilot schools.

Beta vs Full Release Gate

Beta Readiness = staged rollout to 3–5 trusted pilot schools. Lower bar (80) than full release gate (90) because: (1) pilot schools are pre-vetted and aware of beta status; (2) feedback loop is tighter; (3) impact of a bug is contained. Beta launches gate behind feature flag per school. Full Release Gate (≥90) must still be passed before GA rollout.

Sign-off Flow

Three sign-offs required: Owner (final), Eng Lead (technical), Curriculum Lead (content). Each sign-off is a timestamp + email confirmation. Sign-offs are recorded in release_signoffs table and are part of the governance log. Launch button remains disabled until score ≥ 80 AND 3/3 sign-offs.

Beta School Selection

Owner selects beta schools in Settings → Beta Cohort. Beta schools must have signed DPA with beta clause. beta_flag=true in schools table. Beta features are exposed via feature_flags.beta_enabled joined with school context. Schools can be added/removed from beta cohort without a deploy.

Known Issues

Known issues (warnings, not blockers) are listed in the checklist with amber status. These are communicated to beta school admins in the beta launch email. Pilot school admins have a direct Slack channel for beta feedback.
