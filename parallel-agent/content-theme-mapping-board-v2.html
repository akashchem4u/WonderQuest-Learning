Theme Mapping Rules

1. Each skill may be mapped to 1–3 themes. First theme = primary (used for default adventure mode grouping).

2. Unmapped skills appear only in Free Play mode, not in themed adventure modes.

3. Theme assignment does NOT affect adaptive difficulty, skill ordering, or delivery_locked status.

4. ELA skills typically map to Adventure or City Life (story contexts). Science → Nature/Space/Animals/Ocean. Math → Adventure/City Life.

5. Social Studies → City Life preferred. Arts & Music → any theme.

6. Theme content assets (backgrounds, characters) are managed separately in the Asset Library (type=theme_asset).
