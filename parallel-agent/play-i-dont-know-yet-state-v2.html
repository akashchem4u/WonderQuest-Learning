📋 play-i-dont-know-yet-state-v2 · Engineering Spec

Purpose and Philosophy

  • "Yet" is non-negotiable in the button label — growth mindset framing
  • IDK is positioned as a learning strategy ("I need a hint") not a failure mode
  • No star penalty for IDK — child earns full +1⭐ if they get it right after using IDK
  • No penalty counter shown — child does not see how many times they tapped IDK

IDK State Elements (in order)

  • 1. Warm purple (#c9a8ff) IDK banner replaces any existing wrong banner
  • 2. Coach expands to larger card with a bigger, more complete hint than the normal 💡 Hint button gives
  • 3. Question remains active — all answer cards still interactive
  • 4. IDK button becomes ghost/disabled (cannot tap IDK twice)
  • 5. 💡 Hint button still works — can chain IDK → Hint for maximum scaffolding
  • 6. Star-safe shows "IDK costs nothing!" — explicit reassurance

IDK vs. 💡 Hint — Distinction

  • 💡 Hint: Small nudge — "Look at the highlighted part." Minimal scaffolding. First resort.
  • 🤷 IDK yet: Bigger scaffolding — full worked explanation of the strategy needed. Last resort before giving up.
  • IDK hint is more complete and empathetic in tone ("Let's figure it out together")

Post-IDK Correct State

  • IDK banner turns green on correct: "You figured it out — and earned a star!"
  • Coach message explicitly credits the hint: "The hint helped this time. Next time you might remember it without one!" — forward-looking, not praising luck
  • Full +1⭐ awarded — no partial credit, no reduced reward

Language Rules

  • ✅ "That's okay — let's figure it out together!"
  • ✅ "You can still earn a full star!"
  • ✅ "IDK costs nothing!"
  • ❌ "It's okay that you don't know" (condescending)
  • ❌ "You should have studied more" (obviously never)
  • ❌ Any implication that using IDK is cheating or less worthy

IDK Button Styling

  • Default state: ghost button — background: transparent; color: #ff7b6b; border: 2px solid rgba(255,123,107,0.3)
  • Less prominent than the 🔊 Replay and 💡 Hint buttons
  • After tapped: grayed out ghost — color: #5a4878; border-color: #2a2050, disabled attribute
  • IDK button label: "🤷 IDK yet" (mobile) or "🤷 I don't know yet" (desktop)
