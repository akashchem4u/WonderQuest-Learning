💡 Suggestions for this week
Ways to support Maya's learning beyond the app
🎵
Keep the rhyming momentum going!
Maya's rhyming is at 88% — a huge jump this week! Try playing "rhyme games" during car rides: "I say cat, you say a word that rhymes!" Nursery rhymes at bedtime also reinforce these patterns naturally.
📖 Reading · High impact
🔢
Help counting click for Maya
Counting objects is at 60% and hasn't moved this week. Real-world counting helps enormously — try counting stairs, apples at the store, or steps to her bedroom. Touching objects while counting (not just looking) is especially effective for K-age kids.
➕ Math · Building
📚
First words — try pointing to words in books
When reading together, point to simple words like "the", "is", "it", "on" as you read aloud. Ask Maya to spot them on the page. This bridges the WonderQuest practice to real reading — exactly the connection that accelerates progress.
📖 Reading · Building
🌟 Overall this week
Maya showed impressive consistency this week — 5 days in a row is a new personal record! Her engagement is highest in the mornings. If possible, keeping that morning routine stable will compound her progress significantly.
