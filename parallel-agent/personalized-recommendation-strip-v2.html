Data model

personalized_recommendations
Card rows with child_id, type, reason_text, payload JSON, rank, and expiration.
recommendation_impressions
Impression and click logging with route surface and home-load token.
recommendation_suppressions
Per-child suppressions by reason family or card type.

Ranking rules

Interest Match surfaces first when explicit preferences are strong. Warm Replay can outrank discovery after a short gap. Try Next can appear only when the suggestion is still within the child's current band and uses positive framing.

Child route rule

Cards must read as invitations, not obligations. No timers, no "before it disappears," and no performance anxiety copy.

Parent route rule

Every child-facing card needs a corresponding reason row and a dismiss or mute pathway for the parent.
