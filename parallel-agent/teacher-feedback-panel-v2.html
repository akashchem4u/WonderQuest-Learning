ENG NOTE — teacher-feedback-panel-v2.html
Teacher feedback submission panel — teachers can report bugs, request features, flag content issues, or ask questions about the platform.
Feedback types: bug_report | feature_request | content_issue | question.
Routing: All feedback routes to owner feedback workbench. P0 bugs (platform unusable) get immediate Slack alert to on-call. Others queue normally.
Teacher identity: Feedback is tied to teacher account (school, class, role) — helps triage. Teacher name shown in owner workbench but NOT in public bug tracker.
System notifications: Platform update notices, release notes, scheduled maintenance alerts. Teacher reads them here (separate from email).
NPS prompt: Shown in this panel quarterly. Not shown if teacher submitted feedback in past 14 days (avoid survey fatigue).

NPS Survey Rules

NPS/rating prompt shown quarterly. Suppressed for 14 days after any feedback submission (avoid fatigue). NPS score stored by teacher_id — aggregated for product analytics. Individual teacher NPS NOT shown to other teachers. Rating is 1–5 stars (not 0–10 NPS) for simplicity in teacher UI.
