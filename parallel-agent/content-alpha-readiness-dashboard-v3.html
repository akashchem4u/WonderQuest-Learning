Component: content-alpha-readiness-dashboard-v3.html
Aggregate content readiness dashboard. 5 categories (Coverage/Quality/Assets/Review Pipeline/Risk = 100). Thresholds: Alpha ≥70, Beta ≥80, GA ≥90. Per-band readiness table. Active blockers. Content milestones timeline.

Alpha vs Beta vs GA Content Thresholds

Alpha (≥70): Internal testing with teacher testers. Content can have gaps and some blocked skills. Focus on testing the delivery engine and UX, not complete curriculum coverage. Beta (≥80): Pilot schools with real students. Must have minimum viable coverage per band. Blocked skills should be resolved or the affected band marked as limited. GA (≥90): General availability. All bands should have good-to-full coverage. No critical content gaps.

Per-Band Readiness

Each band is assessed independently. P0/P1 may be GA-ready while P3 is still Alpha-only. This allows phased rollout: e.g., open all bands to beta schools but communicate that Grade 4–5 content (P3) is still expanding. Beta launch notes to pilot schools explain content depth per band.

Score Calculation

Coverage (25): weighted average of topic coverage cells across all bands. Quality (25): % of skills with no blocking error reports. Assets (20): % of skills with all assets present. Review Pipeline (20): % of backlogged skills cleared within SLA. Risk (10): deducted for active blocker count and severity. Score is recalculated nightly (cron job) and on-demand when "Refresh Score" is triggered.
