Allowed motion patterns

Snap-in Pieces settle into place with a short ease-out. No bounce that reads as impact.
Guide pulse Outlines glow to suggest the next move rather than shaking the whole scene.
Celebration sweep Confetti and blueprint lines fan outward after success, never upward like an explosion.

Blocked motion patterns

No collapse Wrong answers cannot drop towers, spill materials, or trigger repair sirens.
No collision framing Vehicles may move across the screen, but they cannot bump, smash, or crash into structures.
No countdown pressure Build scenes stay timer-free even in challenge variants.

Publish gate

Every module needs child-safety sign-off, accessibility review, and at least one reduced-motion fallback before it can move from review to published.

Rotation logic

The building pack is eligible when a child has building-style interest tags and no expressed dislike for machine or workshop visuals. Parent pinning overrides automated rotation.

Parent transparency

Recommendation reasons must be visible in plain language: "Suggested because Emma likes blocks and design." No inferred mood targeting or hidden experimentation.
