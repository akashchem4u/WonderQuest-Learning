What is an "intervention": A teacher-acknowledged support queue item becomes an intervention when the teacher taps "View student" and logs a note or takes an action. The intervention tracks: trigger reason | mastery before/after | actions taken | timeline.
Sections: Header (student + trigger) | Mastery progress | Action checklist | Timeline of events | Related skills.
Privacy: First name only, no accuracy % per student, no wrong answers, pattern-level skill context. Teacher notes are private.

What constitutes an "intervention": A queue item becomes an intervention when a teacher opens the detail and takes at least one action (logs note, marks acknowledged, adds action item). System creates an intervention_log record at that point.

Intervention States

StateConditionUI indicator
ActiveTrigger still present; teacher has acknowledgedAmber top border, "Active" chip, mastery delta shown
ImprovingFloor hits decreasing; mastery increasing↑ improving stat; green mastery delta
ResolvedTeacher marked resolved OR mastery crossed 65 with 0 floor hits for 5 daysGreen top border, ✅ icon, resolved date shown
EscalatedNo improvement after 2 weeks; teacher chooses escalateRed top border (this is a system alert, not student-facing)

Action Plan

Teacher can add freetext action items. Each has: title, description, due date (optional), completion checkbox. Actions are private to the teacher. Not shown to parent, child, or other teachers.

Timeline

Automatically records: trigger event, teacher acknowledgement, note logs, mastery changes (status transitions only — not raw score changes), resolution. Does NOT record: specific sessions, question-level data, wrong answer patterns.
