ENG NOTE — teacher-intervention-overview-v2.html
Intervention overview — shows ALL interventions across the class (past and present), not just a single intervention detail.
Distinct from: support queue (shows open items) | intervention detail (shows one intervention in depth) | intervention timeline (shows one intervention's full history).
Route: /teacher/interventions — accessible from command center "Interventions" link or sidebar.
Intervention lifecycle: Active → Improving → Resolved (auto when mastery ≥ 65 + 0 floor hits for 5 days) | Escalated.
Privacy: First names only (FERPA). Teacher private notes visible here (private to teacher). Intervention data retained 90 days then anonymised.
Stats view: Aggregate-only analytics (resolution rate, avg days to resolve, trigger distribution). No per-student comparative ranking.

Retention & Privacy

Interventions retained with full data for 90 days from resolution. After 90 days: student_name anonymised, teacher_notes deleted, aggregate stats retained for product analytics. Teacher cannot export raw intervention data with student names — export is summary-only (totals, trigger counts, resolution rate).
