Privacy in Workbench

Feedback attributed to school name (B2B relationship). Teacher name NOT shown to owner — only school + teacher role (e.g. "Class 4B teacher at Maple Ridge"). This protects individual teacher privacy within the owner analytics view. Teacher identity is stored server-side for response routing but not displayed.

Owner Reply Routing

Owner replies are delivered to the teacher's platform notification panel (not email). Teacher sees: "WonderQuest team replied to your feedback." Reply text shown in teacher's feedback history. No email notification unless teacher has opted into email feedback updates in their settings.
