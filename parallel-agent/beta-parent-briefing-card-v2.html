🔭What is the beta?

WonderQuest is getting ready to launch publicly. Before we do, we're testing it with a small group of real families and schools — just like yours.

Your feedback helps us make sure every child has a great experience when WonderQuest opens to everyone.

🧒What will my child do?

Emma and Noah will play educational quests just like they would in the final product. Everything they see is the real WonderQuest.

The only difference: we'll occasionally ask you for your thoughts on how it's going.

Week & Dates Dynamically populated from user.beta_week and beta_cohorts.week_dates; renders as "Week N of 6 · [date range]"
Child Names Pulled from children table for the authenticated parent; comma-joined if 2+
Language Rendered in parent's preferred language via user.preferred_language; falls back to en
Post-GA Card hidden entirely; replaced with a standard privacy policy link in Settings. Controlled via feature flag beta_briefing_enabled
PDF Action Generates a print-formatted version of the Briefing Card tab; filename: wonderquest-beta-info.pdf
Share Action Copies a pre-filled referral URL with UTM source parent_share; also offers share sheet on mobile
🗄️Content Management
  • Briefing card body text is stored in the CMS and editable without a code deploy
  • FAQ entries are managed through the admin FAQ editor; changes go live immediately
  • Admin can reorder, add, or retire FAQ items without engineering involvement
  • All content supports locale variants; missing locales fall back to en
