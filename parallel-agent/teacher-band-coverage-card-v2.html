ENG NOTE — teacher-band-coverage-card-v2.html
Band coverage card — shows distribution of students across P0/P1/P2/P3 bands.
Embeddable component used in teacher home sidebar, command center, and analytics pages.
Variants: Standard (horizontal bar list) | Expanded drill (click band → shows students in that band) | Wide grid (4-quadrant with student names) | Spec.
Interaction: Click any band row to expand and see which students are in that band (first names only, FERPA).
Privacy: First names only. Status dot only (active/queue). No accuracy %, no parent info.
Band accents: P0=gold #ffd166, P1=violet #9b72ff, P2=mint #58e8c1, P3=coral #ff7b6b.

Interaction — Expand Band

Clicking any band row expands it to show students in that band (first names only, FERPA). Shows: avatar initial, first name, stars this week, status (active/queue). "N more" link loads remaining students inline. Only one band can be expanded at a time — clicking another collapses the previous.

Privacy

Expanded student list: first names only, stars count (whole number), status dot/chip. No accuracy %, no mastery scores, no parent info. Queue indicator (⚠) is acceptable — teacher needs this to cross-reference.
