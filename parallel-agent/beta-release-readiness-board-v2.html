Override Rules
🔐
Owner Override Conditions: Owner can mark as GO with override only if all P0/P1 bugs are resolved AND Privacy dimension passes at 100/100.
📝
Written Rationale Required: Override must include written rationale with a minimum of 50 characters. Short rationales are rejected by the system.
🗃️
Permanent Audit Trail: Override is logged permanently in beta_launch_decisions with is_override=true. Cannot be deleted.
🏫
Commercial Conditional Pass: Commercial Readiness can be conditionally passed if ≥2 LOIs (letters of intent) exist in lieu of signed contracts. LOIs must be on file.
🚦
Overall GO Condition: System auto-approves GO only when all 6 dimensions score at or above their thresholds. Partial scores do not count as a pass for overall determination.
