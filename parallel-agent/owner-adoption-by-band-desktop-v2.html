Component: owner-adoption-by-band-desktop-v2.html
Band-level adoption analytics. MAU, retention, advancement rates per curriculum band. Stacked growth chart. Band advancement funnel.

Band Advancement Definition

A student advances to the next band when mastery_status = Strong across ≥ 70% of the current band's skills AND the teacher confirms readiness. The advancement is recorded in the students table (band_level field). Not time-based — mastery-driven.

Retention Benchmark

Target D7: 80%. Target D30: 70%. P2 leads at 74% D30 — core product-market fit. P3 at 52% is the gap to close with additional content. P0 D30 of 58% is partially explained by lower school device access for Pre-K students.

Privacy

All data is aggregate band/school-cohort level. No individual student data. MAU counts include all students in the band who had at least 1 session in the period.
