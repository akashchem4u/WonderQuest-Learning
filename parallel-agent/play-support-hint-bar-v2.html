Button Structure

  • Hint bar: position: absolute; bottom: 0
  • Padding: 12px 16px 28px (28px = home indicator clearance)
  • Replay: flex: 2 · Hint: flex: 1 · IDK: flex: 1
  • Pre-K: Replay flex: 2 + IDK flex: 1 (no Hint)
  • G2-3 / G4-5: "📖 Re-read" replaces "🔊 Replay"
  • All buttons: height: 52px (min touch target)

Dimming Rules

  • Hint: dims after 1st tap (opacity: 0.35; pointer-events: none)
  • IDK: dims after 1st tap
  • Replay: NEVER dims — always tappable
  • After IDK: Hint also dims (max scaffolding reached)
  • Reset: both re-enable at start of next question
  • ❌ Never gray out Replay — child may need to re-listen

Hint Panel

  • Slides in above button row on Hint tap
  • Animation: translateY(8px) → 0, 0.2s ease-out
  • Background: band-tinted dark (violet #1e1040, mint #0d2018, etc.)
  • Hint text: Nunito 700, 0.84rem, 1.5 line-height
  • Hint icon: 💡 1.4rem, flex-shrink 0
  • Hint dismisses when next question loads (not manually)

Band Color Tokens (Hint Bar)

  • Pre-K: #0e0b00 bg · #ffd166 accents
  • K-1: #0d0a20 bg · #9b72ff accents
  • G2-3: #050f0c bg · #58e8c1 accents
  • G4-5: #110700 bg · #ff7b6b accents
  • All bgs: slightly darker than phone bg for separation

IDK Button

  • Label: "I don't know yet" — "yet" is non-negotiable
  • Two-line label in small button: "I don't / know yet"
  • Triggers IDK state (play-i-dont-know-yet-state-v2.html)
  • Zero star penalty
  • After IDK correct: still earns +1 ⭐
  • ❌ Never "Give up" / "Skip" / "I don't know" (no "yet")

Adaptive Behavior

  • After 2+ Replay taps without answering: show Slow Mode suggestion
  • Slow Mode button appears in hint panel or below Replay
  • (See play-voice-replay-controls-v3.html for Replay details)
  • Hint bar is always visible — never hidden during active Q
  • Hidden during: correct-state overlay, level-up overlay, session complete
