Engineering & product reference
Privacy
School names used in teacher summary (B2B contract). Individual teacher names are NOT shown — school name only.
Student counts are aggregate only — no individual student IDs are displayed or stored in this view.
Teacher NPS Methodology
Same methodology as family NPS. Teacher-specific wording: "Would you recommend WonderQuest to a colleague?"
NPS proxy score = (% Promoters) − (% Detractors). Week 3 result: +61 (Strong).
Per-School Engagement Thresholds
avg_sessions_per_student = total sessions ÷ enrolled students for the school.
Low < 2.0   |   Medium 2.0–3.5   |   High > 3.5
Escalation Flow
Teacher theme → content team escalation creates a review_queue entry with source = 'beta_teacher_feedback'.
Database Schema
beta_teacher_summaries( week INT, school_name TEXT, -- school name, NOT individual teacher name teacher_count INT, student_count INT, session_count INT, avg_sessions NUMERIC, nps_score INT, themes JSONB )
