P0 — Pre-K Starters
4–5 years
P0
  • Max words 12 words per question
  • Answers 2 (preferred) or 4 (simple)
  • Vocabulary Dolch Pre-K sight words + common nouns
  • Concepts Counting 1–10, basic colours/shapes, animal names, simple rhymes
  • Tone Warm, playful; 1–2 syllable words preferred
  • Forbidden abstract concepts multi-step instructions hypotheticals death / illness
P1 — Adventurers
5–7 years · K–1
P1
  • Max words 20 words per question
  • Answers 4 choices
  • Vocabulary Dolch K–1 sight words + subject vocabulary
  • Concepts Addition/subtraction to 20, CVC reading, basic science
  • Tone Encouraging, clear, concrete language
  • Forbidden complex conditionals >2-step reasoning scary content
P2 — Explorers
7–9 years · G2–3
P2
  • Max words 30 words per question
  • Answers 4 choices
  • Vocabulary Grade 2–3 academic vocabulary
  • Concepts Multiplication intro, chapter-book reading level, ecosystems
  • Tone Curious, exploratory; supports multi-sentence questions
  • Forbidden adult social contexts political content extreme weather distress
P3 — Trailblazers
9–11 years · G4–5
P3
  • Max words 40 words per question
  • Answers 4 choices (sometimes 5)
  • Vocabulary Grade 4–5 + subject-specific terms
  • Concepts Fractions/decimals, novel-length reading, historical events
  • Tone Confident, analytical; supports inference questions
  • Forbidden graphic violence inappropriate social themes adult humour
Vocabulary check

Wordlist database mapped to grade levels using Dolch, Fry, and Curriculum Word Lists. Each word is scored by grade level. Words above the band ceiling are flagged amber. Words more than 2 grades above the ceiling are flagged red.

Flesch-Kincaid Reading Level

Formula:

FK = 0.39 × (words / sentences) + 11.8 × (syllables / words) − 15.59

Band targets:

Band FK target
P0≤ 2.0
P1≤ 3.5
P2≤ 5.0
P3≤ 7.0
Content sensitivity

Safe language checker categories are applied at check time. Emotional content is reviewed manually for P0 and P1. Categories include timer language, shame language, exclusionary language, distress-inducing content, and adult social contexts.

Auto vs Manual pipeline
⚡
Auto: Vocabulary check + Flesch-Kincaid score computed automatically on submit.
⚠
Semi-auto: Sensitivity flags are auto-raised; manual confirmation required to resolve.
👤
Always human: Final approval decision is always made by a human reviewer.
Database schema
content_age_checks ( id UUID PRIMARY KEY, skill_id UUID REFERENCES skills(id), question_id UUID REFERENCES questions(id), band_id TEXT, fk_score NUMERIC(5,2), vocab_flags JSONB, sensitivity_flags JSONB, overall_status ENUM('pass','pass_review','fail','pending'), checked_at TIMESTAMPTZ, reviewer_id UUID REFERENCES users(id), reviewed_at TIMESTAMPTZ )
Publish gate
🔒
Age-appropriateness check must be completed (any status) and approved before review_status can advance to Published.
🔄
Re-check trigger: If question text is edited after approval, age check status is automatically reset to pending and must be re-run.
