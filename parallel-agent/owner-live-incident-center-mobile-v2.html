Component: owner-live-incident-center-mobile-v2.html
Mobile incident center. Same 3 states as desktop. Push notification opens directly to active incident view.

Push Notification Behaviour

P0 declaration fires a push notification to the owner's device (FCM/APNs). Tapping the notification navigates directly to the Active P0 view (not the home screen). Notification title: "P0 Incident Declared — [Route Name]". Body: "On-call team paged. Tap to manage."

Layout Differences from Desktop

Desktop: 2-col (timeline + sidebar). Mobile: single-column. On-call team + affected routes collapsed into section cards below the timeline. Add-update form in main content flow (not sidebar). Post-incident condensed to single summary card.

Footer Action Bar

PD link (opens PagerDuty), Resolve button pinned in footer throughout active incident. This ensures the owner can resolve without scrolling to the bottom of the timeline.
