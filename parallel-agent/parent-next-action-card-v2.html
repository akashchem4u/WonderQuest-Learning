ENG NOTE — parent-next-action-card-v2.html
Surfaces 1–3 recommended parent actions based on the child's recent activity, streaks, gaps, and upcoming milestones.
Action types: Encourage (streak at risk) | Celebrate (badge/level-up just earned) | Adjust (session limit change) | Review (weekly report ready) | Setup (linked child needs configuration).
All actions are optional — never use urgent/alarmist language. "When you get a chance" tone.
Parent does NOT need to take any action for the child's progress to continue. Actions are enhancements, not requirements.
