Child missed same question twice. Recovery banner appears below wrong-state answer. Star-safe badge reassures: stars are not decreasing. Two options: "Get a hint" (primary) or "Try again on my own" (secondary). Framing: warm and curious, never shaming.

Trigger Logic

  • Recovery offered after 2nd wrong on same question in same session
  • Simpler Q offered after 3rd wrong (or 2nd wrong after Coach hint)
  • Cross-session: 3+ sessions where same skill was never completed → show recovery at start
  • ❌ Never show recovery after 1st wrong — give child a chance first
  • ✅ Recovery is always opt-in: child can choose "Try again on my own"

Star Safety

  • Stars NEVER decrease during recovery flow
  • Star-safe badge shown on Struggling screen
  • Simpler Q awards full +1 ⭐ on correct
  • Breakthrough awards full +1 ⭐ (not extra — just the normal star)
  • "Extra" chip is motivational text — still only +1 ⭐ value
  • ❌ Never reduce star count anywhere in the product

Language Rules

  • ✅ "Want a little help?" — warm and inviting
  • ✅ "You figured it out!" — celebrates persistence
  • ✅ "Next time you'll know it right away!" — future optimism
  • ❌ Never "You keep getting this wrong"
  • ❌ Never "Still not right" or "Try again" as the main heading
  • ❌ Never "Finally!" or "It took a while but…"

Coach Hint vs IDK Hint

  • IDK hint: child initiates, general tip
  • Recovery Coach hint: system initiates after 2+ wrong, more specific
  • Recovery hint may visually highlight the correct choice direction (subtle, not answer-reveal)
  • IDK never highlights a choice; recovery may
  • Both earn full star on subsequent correct answer

Simpler Q Rules

  • Skill is the same; cognitive load is reduced
  • Reductions: fewer choices (4→2) / add picture cue / shorter sentence / phoneme hint shown inline
  • After simpler Q correct → return to original question (may simplify once more if still wrong)
  • Simpler Q badge: "✨ Let's try an easier one first!"
  • ❌ Never say "You need an easier version"

Breakthrough Celebration

  • Larger correct-state animation than normal
  • Burst rings: 2 rings, staggered 0.15s
  • "+1 ⭐ Earned" chip: star-pop at 0.3s delay
  • Coach cheer shown inline (no full overlay)
  • Full overlay reserved for level-up / badge only
  • Normal star counter bump animation still plays
