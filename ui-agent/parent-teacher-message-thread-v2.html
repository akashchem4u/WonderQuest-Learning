Contact-Safe Messaging

How WonderQuest protects your privacy while keeping you connected.

🛡️
Your contact details stay private
WonderQuest acts as a secure relay between you and Ms. Chen. Your email address, phone number, and home address are never visible to teachers, schools, or other parents.
🔒 What teachers see
Teachers only see your first name and child's name. No email, no phone, no address. All replies go back through WonderQuest.
📨 What you see
Teacher's name and school are visible to you, along with their WonderQuest-issued teacher account. No personal teacher contact details are shared either.

❓ Common Questions

Can Ms. Chen contact me directly?
No. All messages go through WonderQuest and your contact details are never revealed.
Are my messages stored?
Messages are kept for 6 months for your reference, then archived. You can export them at any time from Settings.
What if I need to block a teacher?
You can mute or block any teacher in Settings → Child Settings → Teacher Communications.
