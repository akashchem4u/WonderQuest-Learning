Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Tap "Contact us" in Parent Settings.
Is this comparing Maya to other kids her age? ▼
Never. Everything here compares Maya only to WonderQuest's own internal progression bands — based on how skills develop within our curriculum. No other children's data is involved.
What if Maya is behind the expected band? ▼
WonderQuest has no "expected band" for a given age. Each child's starting point and pace are unique. What matters is consistent forward movement — which we track and celebrate.
How does age vs grade work in WonderQuest? ▼
We use both age and grade as context to personalize the types of scenarios we use — a 7-year-old gets different story contexts than a 5-year-old. But skill progression isn't locked to age or grade. A child can advance faster or slower based on their own practice.
Can I see how Maya compares to her class? ▼
WonderQuest's parent view doesn't show comparisons to other children — individual privacy is important to us. Teachers have a class-level view for instructional planning, but that information isn't shared with other parents.
