    Ask your child to enter their 4-digit PIN on the next screen to confirm their identity.
  2. 2
    If the PIN is wrong or your child doesn't recognise this account, tap "This is the wrong child" to unlink and start fresh.
  3. 3
    Once unlinked, you'll be asked to re-enter your child's name and set a new PIN together.
