Policy: These rules protect parent privacy and ensure all communications remain within WonderQuest's managed system.

Contact-Safe Rules

🔒
No PII displayed. Parent email addresses, phone numbers, and home addresses are never shown in the teacher portal. All contact is anonymous to the teacher.
📨
All messages route through WonderQuest. Messages are delivered by WonderQuest's notification system. Teachers cannot initiate direct email or SMS.
🧾
Full audit trail. Every message is logged with date, teacher ID, student, delivery status, and read receipt. Logs are retained for 12 months.
🚫
No personal information in message body. The system will flag messages containing potential PII patterns (phone numbers, email addresses, home addresses) before sending.
👨‍👩‍👧
Parent opt-in required. Messages are only delivered to parents who have linked their WonderQuest account. Unlinked parents are flagged in the contact log.
⚖️
FERPA compliant. Student data referenced in messages is governed by FERPA. Messages must relate only to the student's academic progress within WonderQuest.
📵
Parent quiet hours respected. WonderQuest honors parent-configured quiet hours. Messages sent outside allowed windows are delivered the next morning.
