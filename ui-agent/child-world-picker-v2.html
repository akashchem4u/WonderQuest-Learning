Tab 5 — Spec Notes
World Naming Rules
Child-facing names are thematic, NOT grade labels. Never show "Pre-K", "K-1", "G2-3", or "G4-5" to the child. World names examples: Dream World (P0), Ocean World (P2), Fire World (P3). Names are defined in content CMS and may vary. Locked world tagline shows "???" until adjacent world is complete.

Grace period threshold (streak reset) is server-configurable. Never hardcode. NEVER say "broke your streak" — always positive framing.
Progress Dots
One dot per skill in the world. Max 10 dots displayed — if world has more than 10 skills, show 10 dots + "…" overflow. Done = bright white filled. Current = bright white with outer glow ring. Remaining = dim outline only.
Bottom Sheet Animation
Sheet slides up from translateY(100%) to translateY(0) in 300ms ease-out. Background cards dim to 30% opacity simultaneously. Dismiss by tapping outside the sheet or swiping down. Locked worlds never trigger sheet — only show toast.
