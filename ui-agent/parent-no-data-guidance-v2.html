Help & Setup

Everything you need to get Mia's learning journey started.

Why isn't my dashboard showing data? ↓
Your dashboard fills in after Mia plays her first WonderQuest session. Until then, it shows a preview of what's coming.
How do I know Mia is using the right account? ↓
When Mia logs in, her profile (name, grade, teacher) should appear. You can verify it matches in Settings → Child Settings.
When will I hear from Ms. Chen? ↓
Teachers usually send a welcome note within the first few days. You'll get a push notification when it arrives.
What if Mia doesn't want to play? ↓
WonderQuest is designed to be genuinely fun — most kids jump right in. Try exploring the different worlds together first!
