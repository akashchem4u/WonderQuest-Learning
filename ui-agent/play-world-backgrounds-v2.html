Star Field Rules
Pre-K: 6–8 stars max, large (3–5px), slow twinkle (3–4s cycle), warm white/gold tint.
K–1: 8–12 stars, medium (2–4px), medium twinkle (2–3s), cool white + violet tint on success.
G2–3: 10–16 stars, small–medium (2–3px), faster twinkle (2s), teal tint on success states.
G4–5: 12–18 stars, small (2px), crisp, minimal tint — more structured/less whimsical.
Stars are CSS-only (no canvas). Position and delay randomised at component mount via JS. Density increases with band level.
Overlay Panel Rules
Bottom-anchored sheet, border-radius: 24px 24px 0 0. Slides up from translateY(100%) to translateY(0) in 400ms ease-out. Backdrop blurs play screen at 30% opacity. Always uses band's accent color for border-top and CTA button. Never show accuracy %, wrong count, or time taken in overlay.
Return Journey Backdrop
Warmer, more saturated version of the default background — signals a special state. Loads before the map renders. Welcome message centered, streak status prominent. CTA is "Start Today's Quest" (not "Resume"). Grace period (streak intact threshold) is server-configurable — never hardcoded in UI.
