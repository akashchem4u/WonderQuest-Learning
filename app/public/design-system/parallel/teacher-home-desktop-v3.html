Privacy Rules (Teacher Context)

Student first names shown — no surnames. No parent contact info visible in teacher portal (separate FERPA boundary). Accuracy % shown as a class-level metric and per-student metric — but in "sessions context" not as a grade. Wrong answer patterns shown at skill-category level only, not per question.
