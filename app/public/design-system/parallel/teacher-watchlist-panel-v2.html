ENG NOTE — teacher-watchlist-panel-v2.html
Teacher watchlist — manual list of students the teacher wants to keep an eye on (separate from system support queue).
Key distinction: Support queue = system-triggered flags. Watchlist = teacher-curated manual list.
Use cases: "Keep an eye on this student for personal reasons" | "Just advanced bands, monitor closely" | "Informal check-in needed" | "Student returning from absence".
Max size: 10 students per watchlist (if > 10, prompt to review/remove).
Privacy: First names only. Watchlist and teacher notes private to teacher only. Not shared with parents or other teachers.
Not alarming: Watchlist is neutral — not a deficit signal. "You're monitoring these students."
