Component: owner-feedback-summary-desktop-v3.html
Aggregate feedback analytics. Distinct from workbench (per-item triage). Period selector: 7d / 30d / 90d. School-level aggregates only.

Data Sources

feedback_items table (submissions) + feedback_metrics table (resolution times, SLA) + nps_responses table (post-resolution surveys). All sourced from analytics warehouse — daily snapshots, not real-time. Real-time counts in workbench only.

NPS Collection

NPS survey sent automatically 24h after resolution (if owner sent a reply). Suppressed for 14 days after previous NPS from same teacher/school. Response is anonymous — owner sees school-level aggregate only.

Top Schools Note

High submission count is NOT a negative health indicator — it often means engaged teachers. School health score is tracked separately in the route health dashboard. The note in the top-schools sidebar panel reinforces this interpretation.

Export

CSV export: raw metrics (item counts, resolution times, SLA %). PDF export: formatted 1-page summary suitable for board/investor report (volume chart, SLA table, NPS score).

Privacy

No individual teacher names in any chart or table. School names shown (B2B contract relationship). Submission content (feedback body text) not shown in summary — only in workbench item detail.
