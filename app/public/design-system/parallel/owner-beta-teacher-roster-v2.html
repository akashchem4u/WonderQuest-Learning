Privacy Rules

School name shown — permissible under B2B contract; no additional consent required.
Individual teacher names NOT shown — only aggregate teacher_count per school is displayed.
Students identified by count only — no student names, IDs, or individual records are exposed anywhere in this view.
FERPA: All student data presented is aggregate only. No individual student records are stored or accessible via this component.

Business Rules

  • 1 Low engagement threshold: avg_sessions_per_student < 2.0 per student per week triggers an amber status flag and "Check-in needed" chip on the school row.
  • 2 Automatic owner notification: When a school crosses the amber threshold, the owner receives an in-app notification and email digest within 24 hours.
  • 3 Hidden after GA: This entire route and component is feature-flagged under beta_teacher_roster_enabled. It is automatically hidden and the route returns 404 once GA is declared.
  • 4 Support log append-only: Support log entries are immutable once submitted. Entries are stored in beta_support_logs with author_id, school_id, and logged_at.
  • 5 NPS display: NPS is computed from feedback_items where item_type = 'nps'. A minimum of 3 responses is required to display a score; otherwise shows "–".
