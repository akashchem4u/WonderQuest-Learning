ENG NOTE — teacher-session-readiness-banner-v2.html
Banner component shown on teacher home during three session lifecycle phases: Pre-session (before students log in), In-session (live — students actively playing), Post-session (after session ends).
Phase detection: Based on sessions_today count and real-time active session count. "Pre" = sessions_today = 0. "Live" = active_sessions_now > 0. "Post" = sessions_today > 0 AND active_sessions_now = 0 (within 2h of last session).
No timer anywhere. The banner never shows countdown timers or time limits.
Live data: In-session banner uses WebSocket / polling (30s) for live student count.
Dismissal: Teacher can dismiss any phase banner. It re-appears on next page load if phase condition still active.
Post-session: Auto-dismissed after 6 hours. Always celebratory — shows wins, stars, and mastery highlights.

Live Data (In-session)

active_sessions_now: count of sessions with last_ping_at within 90 seconds. Refresh: 30s WebSocket or polling. Student count shows currently active sessions — excludes students who played earlier and closed their session.

Post-session Tone

Post-session banner is always positive/celebratory. Shows wins, star totals, mastery count. NEVER shows: accuracy %, wrong answers, queue items (those are in the queue panel separately). Post-session is the "good job today" message for the teacher.
