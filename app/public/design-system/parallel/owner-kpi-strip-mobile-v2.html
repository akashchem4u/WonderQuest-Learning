⚠️ P0 Active — Assignment Engine
Non-dismissible incident banner above strip. Routes tile turns red. Feedback tile turns amber (+2). Period bar shows "10s refresh". Tap the banner to navigate to Incident Center.
P0 banner rules
Banner is non-dismissible while P0 incident is active. Shows incident name + duration + PD ticket ID. Tapping navigates to Incident Center (deep-linked to active incident). Cleared automatically when incident resolves.
Tile colouring logic
Only affected tiles change colour. Routes = red. Feedback = amber (increased due to teacher reports). All other tiles remain white. Do not highlight MAU/MRR/Schools as "affected" — they are unaffected by the incident in real time.
Component: owner-kpi-strip-mobile-v2.html
Horizontally scrollable KPI tile row. 8 tiles total. Period chip scroll. P0 incident banner (non-dismissible). Routes and Gate tiles show text status. Tap to navigate.

Tile Order (Mobile)

Mobile tile order differs from desktop to surface most-actionable metrics first: MAU → DAU → MRR → Schools → Feedback → Routes → Gate → ARR. On desktop, Schools comes after ARR because the wider viewport shows all tiles simultaneously. On mobile, Schools is demoted to position 4 (left-scroll), ARR to position 8 (right-scroll).

Non-Dismissible Incident Banner

When a P0 incident is active, a red banner appears above the period chip row and KPI strip. It cannot be dismissed. It shows: incident name, duration, PD ticket ID. Tapping it deep-links to the Incident Center. The banner auto-clears when incident status changes to Resolved.

Period Selector on Mobile

Period chips are a separate horizontally scrollable row above the KPI strip. MAU and MRR cells don't respond to period selection (always show current month). Changing period re-fetches DAU, Sessions, Feedback, Schools counts for the new window. Tile values update with a brief fade transition.
