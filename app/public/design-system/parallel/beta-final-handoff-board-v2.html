What We Learned — 5 Key Insights

Across all age bands, sessions using the Animal or Ocean theme showed 38% longer average session duration and 22% higher question completion rate compared to the Default theme. Pre-K learners showed the strongest preference for the Animal theme.

Recommendation
Make themes more prominent on the home screen — feature the top 2 themes above the fold. Consider auto-suggesting a theme on first run based on interest capture data.

P0 band relies heavily on audio prompts (letter sounds, counting cues). A 400–600ms audio delay on some Android devices caused confusion and incorrect answers that were scored against learners unfairly. Fixed in v2.5.12 by pre-loading audio assets.

Recommendation
Fixed in v2.5.12. Consider adding a pre-session audio check for parents on first launch (especially on Android). P0 band should always pre-load audio on session start.

Three teachers in the pilot reported that reaching the intervention review queue on mobile took 4+ taps from the home screen. On desktop the shortcut is visible in the sidebar, but the mobile route shell lacked an equivalent entry point.

Recommendation
Shipped: Added a mobile shortcut button in v2.5.11. For GA, consider a persistent bottom-bar badge showing pending queue count on mobile teacher home.

68% of parent respondents said they preferred receiving a weekly email summary of their child's progress over checking the app. Many noted that the in-app notifications are easy to miss when the app is not open. This is especially relevant for parents of younger learners who don't check the app daily.

Recommendation
Adding weekly digest emails for GA launch. Default opt-in. Preferences page will allow frequency settings (weekly / biweekly / off).

The original threshold of 3 consecutive incorrect answers triggered an intervention too frequently, especially during fast-paced play where children made accidental taps. Teachers flagged this as disruptive and children reported feeling "the game keeps stopping."

Recommendation
Recalibrated threshold from 3 → 5 consecutive incorrect answers in v2.5.9. Monitoring data in the first 2 weeks of GA to validate. May need band-specific tuning for P3/P4.
