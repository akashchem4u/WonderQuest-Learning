Critical Play Rules
RuleDetail
CRITICAL No Timer No countdown, no time pressure anywhere in play flow. Stars are the only score metric visible to child.
CRITICAL No Accuracy % Never show "80% correct" or similar. Stars only. Confidence bars shown without % labels (child-facing).
CRITICAL Star-Safe Stars NEVER decrease. Star-safe badge (#1a2a15 bg, #50e890 border) shown on every gap/absent/wrong screen.
CRITICAL Wrong Language "Not quite — give it another try!" or "That wasn't it". NEVER: "Wrong!" / "Incorrect!" / "You keep getting this wrong" / "Finally!"
RULE Boss Fill Boss node segment always fills #ffd166 (gold) on complete regardless of band color.
RULE Reward Overlay NOT shown on every correct answer. Reserved for boss nodes, streak milestones, session bonuses.
RULE Level-Up Timing Level-up triggered at END of session only. Never mid-session. Queued after session-complete overlay.
RULE IDK Button "I don't know yet" — "yet" is non-negotiable. Split to 2 lines in small buttons.
RULE Quit Language Always "Leave" or "Exit". NEVER "Give up" or "Abandon". Quit confirm has intentional hierarchy: Stay primary (right), Leave danger (smaller, left).
RULE Pre-K Hints Pre-K removes Hint button (too abstract). 2-button layout: Replay (flex:2) + IDK (flex:1) only.
Confidence & Recovery Model
StateTriggerAction
Low Confidence Score <30% at session start Offer 3-Q practice: Q1=2 choices, Q2=4 choices, Q3=4 harder. Always opt-in.
Wrong × 2 2nd wrong on same question Show Struggling screen (star-safe badge), coach hint (not answer reveal)
Wrong × 3 3rd wrong on same question Simplify question: 4→2 choices, add picture cue, phoneme hint inline
Breakthrough Correct after scaffolding Larger celebration: burst-ring (2 rings staggered 0.15s), "+1 ⭐ Earned" chip at 0.3s delay
