Stars in Feed Items

Star counts shown in feed are the child's earned stars from that event (badge bonus stars, session stars). These match what the child saw. Stars are never negative and never relate to accuracy.

Pagination

Initial load: 10 items. "Load more" button fetches next 10. No infinite scroll (reduces accidental data exposure). Full history accessible via weekly report or dedicated history view.
