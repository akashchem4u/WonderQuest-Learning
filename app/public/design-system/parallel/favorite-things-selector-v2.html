Data model

favorite_things_profiles
Per-child current favorites array, version, updated_by, and updated_at.
favorite_thing_events
Append-only change log for add, remove, clear, and reorder events.
favorite_thing_catalog
Approved interest options with icon token, display label, and availability state.

Rules

Max 5 favorites. If none are selected, fall back to current theme and onboarding interests. Parent edits take effect immediately for home personalization but never interrupt an active session.

Copy

Use "favorite things" and "likes right now," not "personality type" or "profile score." Parents can change selections any time as the child grows.

Privacy

These preferences stay internal to WonderQuest personalization and are never exposed in teacher or owner views unless aggregated for pack usage analytics.
