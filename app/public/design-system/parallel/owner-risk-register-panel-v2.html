Component: owner-risk-register-panel-v2.html
Risk register. 12 tracked risks. Likelihood × Impact scoring (1–25). Categories: Privacy, Technical, Product, Business, Compliance. Statuses: Open, Monitoring, Mitigated, Accepted. Matrix view (5×5 grid). Owner-only panel.

Risk Scoring

Risk Score = Likelihood (1–5) × Impact (1–5). Score ranges: Critical ≥ 16 (red), High 10–15 (amber), Medium 5–9 (yellow), Low 1–4 (green). Likelihood scale: 1=Rare (once in 3+ years), 2=Unlikely, 3=Possible (once/year), 4=Likely (once/quarter), 5=Almost Certain (monthly+). Impact scale: 1=Negligible (no user impact), 2=Minor (few users), 3=Moderate (school-level), 4=Major (multi-school), 5=Severe (regulatory / full outage / data breach).

Status Definitions

Open: Risk identified, no mitigation controls in place yet. Requires owner action. Monitoring: Mitigation underway or partial controls in place; risk is being tracked actively. Mitigated: Controls implemented and validated. Risk score reassessed (may still be tracked at residual score). Accepted: Owner has acknowledged the risk and accepted it as tolerable at current level. Reviewed quarterly.

Review Cadence

Full risk register review: monthly. Critical risks: reviewed weekly (owner + eng lead). New risks can be added by owner at any time. Risks are never deleted — they are Accepted or Mitigated, then archived after 12 months of Mitigated/Accepted status.

Visibility

Risk register is owner-only. Not visible to teachers, parents, or students. Engineering lead has read access for technical risks. Legal counsel has read access for compliance risks. No individual staff names in risk descriptions.
