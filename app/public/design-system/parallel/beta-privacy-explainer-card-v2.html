⚖️ Legal basis summary
  • Consent
    Feedback submissions, beta journal entries, observations
    User must actively opt in. Withdrawal withdraws consent for future collection; existing data handled per retention schedule.
  • Contract
    Progress data (XP, stars, streak, level), account data
    Necessary for delivery of the service under the Terms of Service agreement.
  • Legit. interest
    Session performance data, crash logs, device and browser information
    Balanced against privacy impact. LIA conducted March 2026 — concluded interest does not override data subject rights given minimal sensitivity and short retention.
  • COPPA
    Parental consent required for all child data collection
    No child under 13 can consent independently. Verifiable parental consent (VPC) gate enforced at onboarding before any child data is collected.
  • FERPA
    School-linked accounts — student records protections apply
    WonderQuest operates as a school official with legitimate educational interest. No student education records disclosed to third parties without prior written consent.
📐 Display rules

  • After GA: this card is replaced by the full production privacy policy. Beta-specific disclosures (crash logs, beta journal entries) no longer apply post-GA.
