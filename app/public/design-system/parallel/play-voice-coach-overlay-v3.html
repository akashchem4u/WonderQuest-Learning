📋 play-voice-coach-overlay-v3 · Engineering Spec

Overlay Anatomy

  • Position: position: absolute; bottom: 0; — bottom sheet over play content
  • Background: gradient fade from transparent top to solid #1a1440 bottom — plays content visible behind (blends, not occludes)
  • Height: auto, max ~200px, padding: 20px 20px 24px

Coach States

  • Idle 44px dot in bottom rail, idle-pulse ring animation, no overlay shown
  • Speaking Overlay slides up, 56px avatar + speech bubble + wave bars, answer cards 35% opacity (listen mode)
  • Celebration Gold border, coach-celebrate scale+rotate animation, gold wave bars, gold speech bubble tint
  • Wrong Support Violet border (not red), encouraging tone, static avatar (not animated), other cards remain active

Wave Bars

  • 5 bars, 4px wide, wave-dance keyframe using --max-h CSS var per bar
  • Each bar has staggered animation-delay for organic feel
  • Speaking state: green (#50e890)
  • Celebration state: gold (#ffd166)
  • Hidden when coach is not speaking

Listen Mode (During Speech)

  • Answer cards: opacity: 0.35; pointer-events: none; while coach speaks
  • Cards restore to full opacity + active when coach finishes
  • This prevents child from tapping an answer without hearing the question

Coach Personas (no visual change — same layout, different emoji + voice)

  • Coach Leo 🦁 — default, warm and encouraging
  • Buddy 🐧 — playful and silly
  • Whisper 🦉 — calm and soft-spoken
  • Zap 🤖 — enthusiastic and energetic

Auto-dismiss

  • Overlay stays visible while TTS is playing (tracked via audio event)
  • Auto-slides down when audio ends
  • Manual dismiss: tap outside overlay or tap avatar
  • Do NOT dismiss mid-speech if child taps a choice — wait for speech to end, then process tap
