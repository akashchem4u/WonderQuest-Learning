Hard Blocker vs Warning

Hard blockers prevent launch entirely. Warnings allow launch but are logged and tracked. If a warning-flagged item is not resolved within 7 days post-launch, it escalates to a blocker for the NEXT release. This prevents warnings from being perpetually ignored.
