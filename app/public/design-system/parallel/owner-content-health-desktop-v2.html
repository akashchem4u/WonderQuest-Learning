Component: owner-content-health-desktop-v2.html
Content quality monitoring dashboard. Shows skill/question health, curriculum review queue, error reports by subject, and band-level summaries.

Content Status Definitions

Published: Live in production, served to students normally. Under Review: Flagged by teacher or auto-detect; skill remains live but is flagged for curriculum team review. Blocked: Locked from delivery; students will not receive this skill until review passes. Blocked can be set manually or automatically (if wrong-answer error confirmed).

Auto-Flag Triggers

Skip rate > 60% → Under Review. Wrong-as-correct reports > 2 → Blocked. Asset 404 detected → Blocked instantly. Session abort mid-skill > 40% → Under Review. All triggers generate a content_errors entry and notify the curriculum lead.

Privacy

All data is aggregate content metrics — no individual student or teacher data. Error reporter shown as "Teacher, [School]" only. Aggregate session counts shown but not linked to individual users.

Blocked Skills in Release Gate

Skills that are Blocked at launch time appear as warnings in the release gate Content category. If > 5 skills are Blocked at launch, it becomes a release gate hard blocker.
