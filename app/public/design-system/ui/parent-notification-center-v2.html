Quiet Hours Rules
Quiet hours suppress all push/SMS (not email). Default: 9 PM – 7 AM all days.
Critical bypass: Broken link, billing, urgent teacher messages always delivered regardless of quiet hours.
Batching: Non-critical notifications fired during quiet hours are batched and delivered at quiet-hours-end.
Multi-child scope: Preferences are per-parent, applied across all linked children. Child-specific preferences not supported in v2.
Parent Language Rules
NEVER show accuracy %, missed-day counts, or backward-looking failure language.
ALWAYS use forward-looking, growth-positive framing: "so close!", "building great habits", "made it to a new world".
Streak missed: If streak breaks, do NOT send "you missed a day". Send nothing, or a positive re-engagement "ready to start fresh?".
