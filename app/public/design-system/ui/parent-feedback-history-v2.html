What Do the Statuses Mean?
Pending

Pending

Your feedback has been submitted and is waiting to be picked up by our team. We typically respond within 2 business days.

In Review

In Review

A member of our team has read your feedback and is actively looking into it. For bugs, this means our engineers are investigating. For suggestions, it means product is evaluating it.

Resolved

Resolved

We've addressed your feedback and shared a response. Check the detail view for what we did and any actions taken. Thank you for helping us improve!

All your feedback is important to us. Even if a suggestion isn't implemented right away, it shapes future updates to WonderQuest.
