Owner Content Health Detail — Spec
Detail view for a single content pack. Route: /owner/content/:id

QA health score formula

Overall = (image_refs × 0.4) + (alt_text × 0.3) + (answer_keys × 0.3)
Recalculates on: every save, nightly scan (6am UTC), manual trigger.
≥95% → green · 80–94% → amber · <80% → red + auto-flag + removed from live rotation.
