📊 Support / Strengths / Next Step Stack Three-cell skill snapshot. Never shows accuracy %. Never shows "missed" framing. Support = skill that needs more reps (positive framing). Strength = skill going well. Next = milestone the child is close to reaching. Desktop: 3-column card grid. Mobile: vertical rows with colored dots.
SNS Stack — Rules
Three-cell summary always shown at top of right-rail on desktop, top of main scroll on mobile.

Cell Rules

CellLabelColorFraming rule
Support⚑ Needs support#ff7b6bPositive: "Aria is still building X" not "Aria failed X"
Strength✓ Strength#2c7a6eCelebratory: "Solid with X — ready to stretch"
Next→ Next milestone#9b72ffForward-looking: "X is the next K–1 goal"

What NOT to show

❌ Accuracy percentages (e.g. "63% correct")
❌ Missed-day counts (e.g. "didn't play for 3 days")
❌ Rankings or comparisons to other children
❌ Raw attempt counts
