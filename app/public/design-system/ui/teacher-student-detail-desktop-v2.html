SNS Tier Display Rules
Flag (🚩): Left-border #9a2a2a; subnav chip bg #9a2a2a. Auto-escalates after 2 consecutive weeks in support tier.
Watch (⚠️): Chip bg #c07800. 1 week in support tier. Check-in recommended.
Strong (⭐): Chip bg #1a8050. Celebrate, no urgent actions.
Tier change logged automatically in intervention history with timestamp and trigger reason.
Intervention Log Rules
Append-only: Notes can never be edited or deleted. Source-of-truth for compliance.
TODAY divider always present. Earlier entries grouped by date.
Log types: Teacher Note (navy), Parent Message (teal), Activity Assigned (violet), Tier Change (red), System Event (grey).
