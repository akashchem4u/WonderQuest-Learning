Variant B outperforms on every measured metric with high statistical significance. The 37pp improvement in engagement rate and 37pp reduction in skip rate represent a meaningful uplift in the product's core reward loop. The "Show Parent" CTA is a standout addition — 42% of Variant B sessions saw a parent CTA tap, indicating this feature drives cross-persona engagement. Ship Variant B to 100% of users.
