Tab Variants

  • After Correct: Gold star chip animates in, loading dots, next-question preview card, progress strip
  • After Hint: Coral star chip, hint-assist badge, owl acknowledgment, no shame messaging
  • Mini-Break Prompt: After 5 questions, owl prompt, stretch vs keep-going choice, stars saved guarantee
  • End-of-Session Preview: Session stats summary, final question card, XP/badge tease

Child Rules Applied

  • NO timers, NO grade labels, NO shame
  • Stars are streak-safe — replaying can't lose stars
  • Hint use is positively framed ("that's learning!")
  • Break is optional — child controls pace
