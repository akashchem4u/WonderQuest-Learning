What is NEVER shown

❌ Correct/incorrect attempt counts
❌ Names of locked badges or trophies
❌ Comparison to other children
❌ Missed-day counts or streaks framed negatively

Stars system

Each completed quest shows 1–3 stars (based on engagement, not accuracy). Stars shown on map under done nodes. Star total shown in topbar. Stars power the overall "level" display — no score, just stars.
