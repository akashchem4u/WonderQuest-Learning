child-streak-saver-overlay-v2 — Specification

Persona: Child · Mobile 375px · Nunito 900 · Overlay · #100b2e bg · #58e8c1 mint for streak

API: GET /child/streak → { current, last_played_at, at_risk, broken } · POST /child/streak/remind-later

  • Streak at risk: urgent but gentle — "Play today to save your streak!" with play CTA + "remind me later"
  • Streak saved: flame animation + "You saved it!" message + XP chip
  • Streak broken: ZERO shame — "Your streak rests" not "You lost your streak". Encourage new streak.
  • New streak started: day-dot row showing 7-day goal, celebration of day 1
  • Replay can never lose stars — streak overlay never mentions star loss
  • No timers, no grade labels, no accuracy %, no punishment framing anywhere
  • Overlay bottom-sheet style with dim background and drag handle
