Before You Start

Run each scenario with your child in natural conditions — at their usual play time. Do not coach or prompt. Just observe and record your notes after.

1
First Session
Onboarding · 10–15 min
Watch your child's first interaction with the app from launch to completing the first question set.
👁️
Watch for: confusion at character selection, unprompted navigation, any dead ends
2
Badge Unlock
Rewards · 5–10 min
Let your child reach a badge unlock moment. Observe their reaction and whether they understand what was earned.
👁️
Watch for: excitement, confusion, re-engagement, or dismissal of the badge screen
3
Streak Saver
Rewards · 5 min
Allow a streak to be about to break and observe whether your child engages with the streak saver prompt.
👁️
Watch for: did they read it? Tap yes/no? Did they feel pressured or motivated?
4
Parent Review
Parent Tools · 5 min
After a session, open the parent dashboard and review your child's session summary. Note what's clear vs. unclear.
👁️
Watch for: can you identify their top skill gap in under 30 seconds?
Scenario 1 of 4
First Session
Onboarding · Estimated 10–15 min · No coaching
Steps
  1. 1Hand device to child with app already downloaded. Do not explain anything.
  2. 2Observe how they launch the app and get through the character selection screen.
  3. 3Watch their path through the first question set — note any taps that go nowhere.
  4. 4When session ends, ask child: "Was anything confusing?" — write down their exact words.
  5. 5Record your observation using the form in the next tab.
What to Watch For
👁️
Confusion at character selection · Unprompted navigation choices · Dead ends or rage taps · Time to first question answered · Verbal reactions
Moderator Tip

Do not intervene. If your child asks for help, say "what do you think you should do?" — observe their next move.
